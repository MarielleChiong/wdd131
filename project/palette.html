How to Use the Color Palette Generator

  • Start with 3 random colors when the page loads.
  • Click Add Slot to add more colors.
  • Click Remove Slot to delete the last one.
  • Use Generate Colors to refresh all current slots.
  • Click Save to move a color to your saved section.
  • Saved colors stay until you remove them manually.

Tip: We’re working on saving your palette even after refresh—stay tuned!
